Sketchy Magic

Description

Welcome to Sketchy Magic. In this game, you have to protect your newly found treasure by drawing shapes to cast magic.

Controls

To cast spells in this game, use the mouse to draw shapes on the screen. There are currently 2 spells available to you.

  • Triangle

    By drawing a triangle on the screen, you gain the ability to summon an icy triangle the freezes all that it touches.

  • Line

    The line spell punishes anyone that wanders in it's path. Explosions run along the path you create.
